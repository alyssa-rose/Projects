ABOUT

Hey! I'm Alyssa Rose, a current 3rd year undergrad at Tufts University studying Data Science in the School of Engineering (SOE) along with minors in Applied Mathematics and Arabic (Modern Standard).

You can find a few of my projects here from personal, academic and work endeavors, along with a few recognitions of my achievements, including from Secretary of State Michael R. Pompeo. Links to a few of this projects may be missing, including a paper (Unclassified) co-authored with The Department of Defense, Army Futures Command and my teammates at Columbia University and Tel Aviv University which will be published at the end of this month.

Feel free to add me on LinkedIn and check out my GitHub.
